Curricular Practical Training (CPT)

Eligibility

Curricular Practical Training (CPT) is employment authorization for F-1 students participating in a required internship, cooperative education, or other practicum that is an integral part of the established curriculum. You must have been enrolled full-time in F-1 status for at least one full academic year before you are eligible, with a few program-specific exceptions.

Course registration

CPT must tie to a specific course that requires or grants credit for the off-campus practical experience. You cannot begin working before the CPT start date approved on your updated Form I-20.

Part-time vs. full-time

Part-time CPT is 20 hours per week or less and can be used during the academic year. Full-time CPT is more than 20 hours per week and is generally used during breaks. Twelve months or more of full-time CPT eliminates OPT eligibility.
