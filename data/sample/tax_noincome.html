Tax Filing for F-1 Students with No U.S. Income

Form 8843

All F-1 students who are nonresident aliens for tax purposes must file Form 8843 with the IRS for each year they are physically present in the United States, even if they had no U.S. source income during the year.

Deadline

If you had no U.S. source income, Form 8843 is due by June 15 of the following year. If you had U.S. source income, the normal tax filing deadline of April 15 applies.

Where to get help

Georgetown OGS provides access to the Sprintax software each spring for current F-1 students to prepare their nonresident tax filings. OGS cannot provide individual tax advice; questions about your specific situation should go to a qualified tax professional.
