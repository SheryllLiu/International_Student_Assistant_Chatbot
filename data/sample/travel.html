Travel and Re-entry for F-1 Students

Required documents

To re-enter the United States in F-1 status you should carry: a valid passport (generally valid at least six months beyond the date of re-entry), a valid F-1 visa stamp, and a Form I-20 with a current travel signature from your OGS advisor.

Travel signature

The travel signature on page 2 of your I-20 is generally valid for 12 months while you are a continuing student, and 6 months while on OPT or STEM OPT. Request an updated signature from OGS before you travel if your current signature is about to expire.

Traveling during OPT

If you travel during post-completion OPT you should also carry your EAD card and evidence of employment or a job offer. Travel while your OPT application is pending is risky and not generally recommended.
