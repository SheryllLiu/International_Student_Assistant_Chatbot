F-1 On-Campus Employment

General rules

F-1 students in valid status are eligible to work on campus. On-campus employment does not require a separate work authorization document, but you must maintain full-time enrollment and valid F-1 status throughout the job.

Hours limits

During the fall and spring semesters, F-1 students may work up to 20 hours per week in total across all on-campus jobs. During official school breaks and the annual summer vacation, F-1 students may work more than 20 hours per week on campus, up to full time.

Social Security Number

You will need a Social Security Number (SSN) to be paid for on-campus work. OGS provides a letter template for applying to the Social Security Administration after you have a signed job offer.
