Post-Completion Optional Practical Training

When to apply

You may apply for post-completion OPT up to 90 days before your program end date and no later than 60 days after it. OGS must recommend OPT in SEVIS and issue an updated Form I-20 before USCIS will accept your application.

Application timing

USCIS must receive your Form I-765 application within 30 days of the OGS OPT recommendation in SEVIS. Plan to file well before your program end date because approval typically takes two to five months.

Employment rules on OPT

You may not begin working until you have the EAD card and the employment start date on the card has arrived. During post-completion OPT you are limited to 90 days of unemployment. Employment must be in a field directly related to your major.
